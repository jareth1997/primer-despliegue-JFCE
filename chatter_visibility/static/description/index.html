CORE FEATURES

Powerful Capabilities

Everything you need for workspace customization

Instant Visibility Toggle

Show or hide the chatter panel with a single click. Perfect for maximizing your workspace when you need to focus on form data without distractions.

Click the chatter button in the navigation bar to toggle visibility instantly.

Flexible Positioning

Move chatter to Right (default) or Bottom position. Choose the layout that works best for your workflow and screen size.

Select your preferred position from the dropdown menu in the control panel.

Auto-Save Preferences

Your settings are automatically saved in browser storage and restored every time you open a form. No manual configuration needed.

Preferences persist across sessions using browser localStorage.

Beautiful UI

Clean, intuitive dropdown menu with smooth transitions. Integrated seamlessly into Odoo's control panel for a native experience.
